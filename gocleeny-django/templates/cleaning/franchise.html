Investment & Returns

Initial Investment

The initial franchise fee is £15,000, with a total investment ranging from £25,000 to £50,000 depending on your location and scale of operations.

What's Included

  • Exclusive territory rights
  • Comprehensive training program
  • Marketing materials and support
  • Proprietary cleaning methods and eco-friendly product access
  • Business management systems
  • Ongoing operational support

Potential Returns

While individual results may vary, our established franchisees typically achieve profitability within 6-12 months, with potential annual revenues of £100,000 to £500,000 depending on territory size and business development.
