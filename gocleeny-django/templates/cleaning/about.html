About GoCleeny

We're on a mission to provide eco-friendly cleaning services that make a difference.

Our Story

GoCleeny was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide exceptional cleaning services while minimizing our environmental footprint. As part of the SAMBIC Group, we leverage years of industry experience to deliver cleaning solutions that are both effective and eco-conscious.

We believe that a clean home shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. That's why we've carefully selected cleaning products and methods that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment.

What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted cleaning service provider, but our commitment to personalized service and environmental responsibility remains unchanged.

Our Values

Care

We care deeply about our customers, our team members, and our planet. Every decision we make is guided by this principle.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from the products we use to how we manage our operations.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every cleaning job, no matter how big or small. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
